Gala Dinner

On Thursday, September 25, 2025, we have the pleasure of inviting you to the Gala Dinner at LUMOAMO, at the Lapland Hotel Arena.

Lapland Hotel Arena offers memorable experiences in the Tampere city centre. Located at the modern Nokia Arena, it combines the best urban services with a fresh, Northern spirit.

Located on the 5th floor, the party world LUMOAMO was created to enrich the most enchanting moments of events. The place is complemented by the diverse viewing lounge areas that can be combined in a flexible way as well as the unique experiences offered by the restaurant.

The joy and respect for Nordic cuisine extends from the kitchen to the restaurant, bar and lounge. The restaurant appreciates its northern heritage and has brought the purest ingredients from the north: game, reindeer, mushrooms and wild berries.
